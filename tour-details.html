Europe Tour Packages from Bangladesh: Explore the Rich Cultural Heritage and Breathtaking Scenery

Are you dreaming of an unforgettable journey through the historical landmarks, picturesque landscapes, and vibrant cities of Europe? Look no further! Europe offers a mesmerizing blend of ancient traditions, architectural marvels, and natural wonders that will leave you in awe. With our specially curated Europe tour packages from Bangladesh, you can embark on a remarkable adventure that will create memories to last a lifetime.

Discover the Charm of Europe

Exploring the Historical Marvels

Uncover the secrets of Europe's captivating history as you wander through its ancient cities and landmarks. Marvel at the grandeur of Rome's Colosseum, a testament to the might of the Roman Empire.Discover the glamorous French Riviera and step into the enchanting world of Athens and witness the awe-inspiring Parthenon, standing tall on the Acropolis hill. Immerse yourself in the fairytale-like atmosphere of Prague's Old Town Square, adorned with stunning Gothic and Baroque architecture.

Indulge in the Cultural Delights

Europe is a treasure trove of diverse cultures and traditions. Delight your senses with the rich flavors of Italian cuisine in Rome, where you can savor authentic pasta and mouthwatering gelato. Lose yourself in the enchanting melodies of classical music in Vienna, the birthplace of Mozart and Beethoven. Experience the vibrant nightlife of Barcelona, where the streets come alive with passionate flamenco performances.

Itinerary:

Day 1: Dhaka - Paris

Arrive Paris international airport, transfer to hotel, and rest of the day free at your own leisure. Overnight at hotel.

Day 2: Paris-Lucerne, 650KM (B)

Enjoy a scenic journey to the Swiss Alps from Paris. At the picturesque city of Lucerne, you’ll get to visit the Chapel Bridge and the Water Tower, with free time to shop or take pictures. Stop At: Chapel Bridge, Lucerne After boarding the luxury coach in Paris, sit back and enjoy a scenic journey across classic French countryside en route to mountainous Switzerland. You’ll pass through Basel, which is located at the point where the Swiss, French and German borders meet, before arriving at the picturesque lakeside city of Lucerne, nestled at the foot of the snow-capped Swiss Alps. Here you’ll get to see Lucerne’s famous landmarks, including Chapel Bridge, the Water Tower, and some wonderfully preserved Baroque and Gothic architecture, followed by some free time to shop or take pictures. Duration: 1 hour Overnight in or outside Lucerne
