About Us

Muniba Travels is a travel agency based in Dhaka, Bangladesh, offering services such as visa assistance, bank sponsorships for students and visitors, and support for individuals aiming to study or visit abroad. They are located at 43, Road-03, Sector-03, Uttara, Dhaka.

For more information or assistance, you can contact them via WhatsApp at +880 1756 082993.

They maintain an active presence on social media platforms, including Facebook and Instagram, where they share updates and information about their services.
